A — onBlur closes dropdown, "+ SELECT" button per result

Autocomplete closes on blur (click-away). Each flight row has an explicit "+ SELECT" button that turns "✓ ADDED" on click and sends the leg data to TripPlanner. Simple, clear affordance.

B — Radio-select row, single "Add to Plan" CTA

Click the whole row to select it (radio button style). One "→ ADD SELECTED LEG TO PLAN" button at the bottom commits it. Familiar pattern, prevents accidental adds.
