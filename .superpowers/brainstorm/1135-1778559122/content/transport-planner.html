Design decisions

1 — Destination first

TO field on top. User commits to where they're going before filling origin — mirrors how people actually plan. FROM auto-populates from GPS as nearest airport/station.

2 — Location-aware origin

GPS resolves nearest hub (airport, train station, ferry terminal) based on active mode. Shows "● USING YOUR LOCATION" indicator. One tap to override if wrong.

3 — Filters: structured + tag

4 grid dropdowns for Leave By, Arrive By, Max Price, Sort By. Tag pills below for quick boolean toggles: Direct only, Eco priority, Flexible ticket, Cabin baggage only. Filters live-update results.

4 — Radio-select + expand

Click row → selects it (radio dot fills). Selected row expands to show departure/arrival times, baggage, flexibility, CO₂ delta vs cheapest. "→ ADD TO PLAN" appears only in expanded state.

5 — ADD TO PLAN feeds RouteMap

Adding a leg writes to useTripStore (from, to, mode, times, price). RouteMap, ElevationStrip, and TimelinePath all read from the same store — they update automatically.

6 — Mode tabs carry filters

Each mode (Flight/Train/Bus/Ferry/Drive) shows mode-appropriate filters. Drive shows toll roads toggle + fuel type. Ferry shows cabin class. Train shows rail pass option.
