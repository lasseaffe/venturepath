# Comparison deep-dives

Each link below opens a dedicated comparison with feature breakdowns, pricing math, FAQ, and a decision flowchart.

### [VenturePath vs Wanderlog →](/compare/wanderlog)

The free planner with unlimited stops. Best free tier on the market for solo trips. The honest case: if you're solo on a budget and don't need Squad voting or Tactical Mode, use Wanderlog.

### [VenturePath vs TripIt Pro →](/compare/tripit)

The email-magic OG. Still the gold standard for points-and-miles business travelers — and still without a map, real-time collaboration, or offline mode in 2026. Pricing math: $39/yr vs $49/yr, and Pioneer Pass adds everything TripIt left on the table.

### [VenturePath vs Mindtrip →](/compare/mindtrip)

The AI-chat-meets-map planner that acquired Thatch in March 2025. The most strategic comparison if you're a creator wondering where to host your travel guides post-acquisition. Founding-Architect status (waived revenue share, 12 months) is on the table.

### [VenturePath vs Polarsteps →](/compare/polarsteps)

The auto-GPS travel diary. Best post-trip product on the market. The most counterintuitive comparison: we recommend you use *both* — Polarsteps for the diary, VenturePath for the plan. Different jobs.

# What VenturePath is and isn't

**VenturePath is built for:**
- Squads of 3+ Pioneers who argue about restaurants, stops, and budgets.
- Trips into places where signal is unreliable or safety is a real concern.
- Architects who want to publish forkable Pro-Paths and earn revenue share on clones.
- Travelers who want their Expedition's plan, survival, and post-trip story in one app.

**VenturePath is *not* the right pick if:**
- You're solo, budget-tight, and only need a free shared list. Use [Wanderlog](/compare/wanderlog).
- You fly weekly for business and live in your points/miles balance. Use [TripIt Pro](/compare/tripit).
- Your entire need is a beautiful effortless diary with year-end Spotify-Wrapped vibes. Use [Polarsteps](/compare/polarsteps) (or pair it with us).
- You need the deepest AI + map + booking integration *today* (we're catching up — verify our P0 status when you read this). Try [Mindtrip](/compare/mindtrip).

We'd rather you find the right tool than sign up for the wrong one.

# Frequently asked questions

**How often do you update these comparisons?**
We re-verify feature matrices and pricing quarterly, and within seven days of any major competitor launch, acquisition, or pricing change. Each page's "Last verified" timestamp at the top is the source of truth.

**Are these comparisons sponsored by the competitors?**
No. VenturePath maintains them as part of our marketing. We disclose this at the bottom of every page and don't accept money to feature or downplay competitors.

**What if I think a comparison is wrong?**
Email lasse@venturepath.com with a source. If we agree, we'll correct it within 7 days and credit you if you want. Our credibility depends on these being right.

**Do you compare against Roadtrippers, Tripsy, Travefy, or others?**
We started with the four most-searched competitors (Wanderlog, TripIt, Mindtrip, Polarsteps). Additional comparisons (Roadtrippers, Tripsy, Travefy, Komoot for outdoor) are on our roadmap. If there's a specific competitor you want covered, email us.

Yes — the Free Architect tier includes one active Expedition with unlimited Squad collaborators, VentureVault browse, and basic Tactical Mode. Pioneer Pass ($5.99/mo · $39/yr) unlocks unlimited Expeditions, full offline, Trip Reel, and Magic Camera.
